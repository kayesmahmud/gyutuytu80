## 🎯 Which Option Should You Choose?

### ✅ Choose Auto-Detection If:
- You want the smoothest user experience
- Most users won't need to switch languages
- You want zero friction on first launch

### ✅ Choose First Launch Picker If:
- Language choice is critical for your users
- You want to make users explicitly aware they can choose
- You want a more "formal" onboarding

### ✅ Add Header Toggle If:
- Users frequently switch between languages
- You have bilingual users (many Nepalis speak both)
- You want quick access without going to Settings
